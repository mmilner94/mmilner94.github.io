Me, In a Nutshell

I am a screenwriter, podcaster, and Co-Founder of The Write or Die Chicks, an Orange County based collective for emerging female screenwriters looking to find their voice, find a friend, or just find accountability for their writing.

I love talking about the screenwriting process and encouraging people to pursue creative ventures. You can think of me as a sort of creativity bully, in that sense. I’ll push you, but only because I believe in your potential. Everyone has a story, and I want to know what it is! At the very least, I want you to know what it is and indulge in the process of releasing it on to paper.

Let me help you find your voice, and in return I’ll lend you my eyes, ears, and heart.
